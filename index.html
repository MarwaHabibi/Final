Who Am I?

I am a passionate young Afghan leader, writer, and researcher dedicated to advancing education, gender equality, and human rights. Currently, I am training as a Full Stack Developer, honing my skills in both frontend and backend web development. Alongside programming, I possess strong capabilities in leadership, project management, and digital tools. Fluent in multiple languages, I focus on social entrepreneurship, peacebuilding, and girls’ education. Through my writing, research, and advocacy, I strive to create positive social impact and contribute to global development. I am eager to collaborate on opportunities that support personal and professional growth and drive meaningful change.
